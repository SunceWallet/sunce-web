Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2025

At Sunce Wallet, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Stellar wallet application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Wallet Data: Public keys, transaction history, and account balances (stored locally on your device)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for support purposes
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve our services
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting customer support

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide wallet functionality and transaction processing
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Private keys and sensitive data are stored only on your device
  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Biometric Security: Touch ID and Face ID for app access
  • Hardware Security: Secure enclave integration where available

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Stellar Network: Direct interaction with the Stellar blockchain
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics (with opt-out available)
  • Push Notifications: Transaction confirmations and security alerts

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of analytics and marketing communications
  • Export your wallet data

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Transaction history is maintained locally on your device and can be deleted at any time. Support communications are retained for 2 years unless deletion is requested.

Children's Privacy

Sunce Wallet is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

Sunce Wallet is available globally. If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers are located.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of Sunce Wallet after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
